# Problem Solved

## Context
High inbound case volume, low first-contact resolution, and repetitive customer questions driving SLA breaches and support cost.

## Baseline Pain Points
- High manual workload and operational inconsistency
- Limited observability into process bottlenecks
- Weak governance around cross-module handoffs

## Target Outcomes
- Ticket deflection rate >= 30%
- FCR +12%
- MTTR -18%
- Knowledge helpfulness >= 85%

## Measurement Plan
- Define baseline metrics for 8-12 weeks before rollout.
- Instrument post-release dashboards by team and business service.
- Review KPI trends in weekly service governance forums.
